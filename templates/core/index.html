Savory Sunday

Kick off the week with a burst of sweet and savory flavors! Indulge in sweet delights and pair them with creamy milkshakes. Satisfy your cravings with chocolate, strawberry, or vanilla flavors. Elevate your Sunday indulgence with our delectable treats and refreshing milkshakes.

Meaty Monday

Start your day with a protein-packed day! Sink your teeth into tender and juicy steak, expertly cooked to perfection. Choose from a selection of prime cuts, such as ribeye or filet mignon, bursting with flavor. Accompanied by delectable sides and sauces.

Taco Tuesday

Spice up your Tuesday with a fiesta of flavors! Build your own tacos with a variety of fillings like grilled shrimp, seasoned ground beef, or marinated grilled chicken. Top them off with fresh salsa, guacamole, and a sprinkle of cheese.

Wholesome Wednesday

Enjoy a colourful salad bursting with fresh green, vibrant veggies, and grilled chicken or tofu.For a healthier option, try a bowl of quinoa or a veggie stir-fry.

Traditional Thursday

Dive into flavors of Kenyan cuisines. Go back to your roots with hot white or brown ugali served with a side of sukuma-wiki, wet-fried beef and kachumbari.

Flavour Friday

Indulge in the tantalizing flavors of perfectly fried fish and the satisfying crunch of golden chips with a complementary 500ml soda.

Seafood Saturday

Celebrate the end of the workweek with a seafood extravaganza! Savor succulent shrimp scampi, grilled salmon, or a seafood paella brimming with clams, mussels, and calamari. Don't forget a squeeze of lemon to enhance the flavors.
